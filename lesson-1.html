Welcome to the vocabulary class

Welcome to your online english class. In this class you will learn more about vocabulary.

What is vocabulary? Well vocabulary is the body of words used in a language.

Why is vocabulary important? Vocabulary plays a very important role in reading.

The best way to improve on our vocabulary is by reading, using a dictionary or practicing new words while talking.
