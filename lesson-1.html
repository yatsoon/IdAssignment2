Here you will learn more about vocabulary

What is vocabulary?

Well vocabulary is the body of words used in a language

The best way to improve on our vocabulary is by reading, using a dictionary or practicing new words while talking.

Here are a list of short stories that you may read in order to learn more vocabulary

  1. My name is James. I am eight years old. This is my bag. I have two pens, three pencils and one ruler. I am sitting on my chair in my classroom.
  2. My name is Johnny. I eat breakfast in the morning, lunch during the afternoon and dinner at night.
  3. A bakery bakes a small cake to feed a few people. A big cake is used to feed alot of people. A small cake is light in weight. A large cake is heavy in weight
  4. My name is Mary. I use a knife to spread butter on bread. I scoop some icecream and put it on bread. I sprinkle my favourite rainbow sprinkles on the icecream.
  5. The bird is flying in the air. The lion is walking on land. The dolphin swims on the ocean.
  6. The dog's baby is called puppy. The chicken's baby is called chick. The sheep's baby is called Lamb.
  7. Fishes can be found in the river. Monkeys can be found in the jungle. Birds can be found flying in the air or on the trees.
